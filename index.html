Scientific Data Visualization

Scientific data visualization uses graphs, charts, and images to communicate scientific research. There are many different ways to represent data and it is important to choose the right graph and chart types that make the data easier to understand. Poor data visualization choices can lead to designs that confuse your audience or make them ignore the results of your research.
